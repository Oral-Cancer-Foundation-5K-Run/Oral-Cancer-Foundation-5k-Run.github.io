About the Oral Cancer Foundation

The Oral Cancer Foundation is a national public service designed to reduce suffering and save lives through prevention, education, research funding, advocacy, and patient support activities.

Approximately 58,590 people in the US will be newly diagnosed with oral cancer in 2024. Oral cancers include salivary gland cancers, tongue cancers, and more.

This site provides information about symptoms, treatments, and more to help raise awareness and combat oral cancer.
